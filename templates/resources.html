Caregiver Support

Support Groups

The Alzheimer's Association provides free support groups for caregivers. These groups offer emotional support, advice, and community.

Helpline Services

Call 1-800-272-3900 for the Alzheimer's Association 24/7 helpline. Trained staff can answer questions and provide guidance.

Medical Professionals

Consult with healthcare providers, geriatric specialists, and neurologists for medical advice and treatment options.

Educational Resources

Webinars & Workshops

Many organizations offer free online learning sessions about Alzheimer's disease, caregiving tips, and symptom management.

Research Articles

Access peer-reviewed scientific research and clinical studies about Alzheimer's disease on NIH and medical journal websites.

Awareness Campaigns

Organizations run awareness campaigns during Alzheimer's Awareness Month (June) with educational events and resources.
